Strategic Insights

Google — the universal channel

Google appears in virtually every path as both opener and closer. Its dual role means it earns credit in both first-touch and last-touch models, making it the highest-value channel regardless of attribution methodology.

GoogleHotelAds — undervalued assister

With a high assist ratio (~65%+), Google Hotel Ads frequently surfaces in discovery paths but rarely closes alone. Last-touch models significantly undervalue its contribution to revenue — consider data-driven attribution.

(direct) — high-intent signal

Direct traffic almost exclusively acts as a last-touch converter, indicating returning visitors with strong purchase intent. These are likely loyalty or CRM-driven customers already familiar with the brand.

Social (FB/IG) — awareness only

Facebook, Instagram, fb, and m.facebook.com consistently appear in early path positions with high assist ratios and long days-to-conversion (7+ days). Optimise for reach and awareness KPIs, not last-touch ROAS.

Affilired — mid-funnel bridge

Affilired consistently appears between Google and the final conversion step. It plays a coupon/deal surfacing role — customers discover it mid-research and use it to validate pricing before converting elsewhere.

Email — long-cycle nurture

Email paths average 6+ days to conversion with multi-touch sequences. The r.email.myghs.com redirect tracker dominates, suggesting email consistently warms leads that eventually convert through Google or direct channels.
